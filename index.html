Fast Words

Rules

  • Use the 20 random letters at the bottom.
  • Drag letters anywhere in the black grid.
  • Click on a letter to put it back in the bottom.
  • Build words horizontally or vertically, not diagonally.
  • Pause the game for any time you need.
  • Time runs after 5 minutes.
  • End game before and earn 1 point per 10 seconds left.
  • Use at least 10 letters to end before time runs up.
  • iOS validates if words are valid or not.
  • Word validation occurs when game ends.
  • Letter's borders are colored at game end.
  • Correct words are bordered green, incorrect red.
  • Gain points for correct words.
  • Lose points for incorrect words.
  • Letters not used will not add nor subtract points.
  • The more rare the letter, higher the point value.
