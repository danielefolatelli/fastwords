Fast Words

Rules

  • Use the 20 random letters at the bottom to build words.
  • Drag letters anywhere in the black grid.
  • Use the arrows at the bottom right to move the whole grid.
  • Letters must form a crossword, if not they will be outlined in red.
  • Build words horizontally or vertically, not diagonally.
  • Words are valid from left to right or from top to bottom.
  • All words must be connected forming a crossword.
  • If a letter is no longer needed drag back to the bottom.
  • Time runs up after 5 minutes.
  • You can pause the game, you will not see screen while paused.
  • End game by clicking Stop button to earn 1 point per 10 seconds left.
  • Must use at least 10 letters to end game before time runs up.
  • Word validation and scoring occurs when game ends.
  • iOS validates if words are valid or not.
  • If words are not connected, the game is not valid and no scoring occurs.
  • Any loose letters on the grid are removed at game end.
  • Correct words are outlined green, incorrect words red.
  • Gain points for correct words.
  • Lose points for incorrect words.
  • Letters not used will not add nor subtract points.
  • Letters are colored according to point value, as shown in the bottom right.
  • Double score for correct words with 6 or more letters.
  • Double score words have blinking outline.
  • Scoring for each word will be shown by a number next to the word.
  • The arrow besides word scores indicates if word is horizontal or vertical.
  • One thick white border on word scores touches the corresponding word.
  • Click on any score and the word's letters will blink.
